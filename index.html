about

Welcome to my website! I am Ned Ellis, a student at the University of Illinois currently getting my B.S. in Computer Engineering. My main area of focus is currently embedded systems with aerospace applications but I also have a passion for "automating the boring stuff" with Python, be it regular CPython, or more recently, MicroPython. In the past I have also worked with Autonomous Drones, Robots, Medical Imaging Systems, NLP text classification and many, many other side projects. Sadly I do not have enough time to keep this blog up to date on a regular basis but check back semianually to see what I am up to!

Check me out on Github.
