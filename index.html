about

Welcome to my website! I am Ned Ellis, a student at the University of Illinois currently getting my B.S. in Electrical Engineering. Historically, I've been working with a lot of embedded systems, mostly for robotics or consumer electronics. One of my focuses right now is understanding how sensors are used in platforms to determine position and state. I have also been working on some personal projects like making a Spotify connected visualizer and an electric skateboard.
